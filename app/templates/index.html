Welcome to the School of Dandori

Reclaim Your Time. Rediscover Your Joy.

In a world increasingly dominated by machine intelligence and constant digital noise, the School of Dandori offers a sanctuary for the human spirit. Founded in 2017 by technologists Ada Calm and Tessa Forman, our school was born from a simple but powerful belief: as machines master our attention, humans must work to reclaim it.

Dandori is the art of managing resources efficiently to maximize output. At our school, we believe your most precious resources are your time and your wellbeing.

Our philosophy is centered on the idea that life should be enjoyed. We encourage you to:

  • Reconnect with your younger, more carefree self
  • Disconnect from the relentless stresses of modern life
  • Embrace your most playful nature through the "whimsy" of our curriculum

In today's fast-paced world, we often forget to pause and enjoy the simple pleasures. At Dandori, we create spaces where adults can rediscover the joy of learning without pressure, deadlines, or expectations. Each class is an invitation to slow down, be present, and reconnect with what truly matters.
